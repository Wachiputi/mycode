Malawi The Warm Heart Of Africa

Malawi, the so called warm heart of africa is one amongst many land locked countries
foung no where else in the universe but plannet earth. it is found in the southern part of continent Africa covering an area of 54 800
square kilometers at an altitude of 890 m above the see level. Malawi has 28 district which are devided into three regions.The nothern
region cover 7 of the districts which are, Chitipa, Karonga, Rumphi, Mzimba, Nkhata-bay, Likoma and .... The central region is composed
of Lilongwe, Kasungu, Ntcheu, Salima, Ntchisi, Dedza, Dowa, Nkhotakota, Balaka, Dwangwa and Southern region covers Blantyre, zomba,
Mangochi Machinga Mulanje Thyolo Phalombe Neno Mwanza

Agriculture

Agriculture ia one of the most valuable industry in the country.This is done both at small scale
as well as at large scale.The economy of the country heavily relies on agriculture through cash crops like tobacco,tea and cotton.
The country also produces food crops like peas,millet,sorghum,corn,nuts,oranges.mangoes,pineapples jus to mention a few.The soils
are rich in plant nutrients thus from alluvial soils to iron rich soils, supporting the cultivation of various crops in the country
